About Me

I am Matyas Kounovsky, an independent developer and hardware designer. Merix Industries serves as my professional platform for delivering bespoke software and hardware solutions designed to meet the rigorous demands of modern technology.

With an extensive background in Python, Java, and C++, alongside specialized expertise in low-level PCB design for embedded systems, I focus on bridging the gap between complex concepts and functional reality. I believe that engineering is not merely about writing code, but about creating efficient tools that empower businesses to grow.
